Join Vassar's largest Asian identity organization!

Vassar’s Asian Students’ Alliance is a student-run organization (org) that aims to be a community and resource for Asian and Asian American students. ASA hosts weekly meetings for its members and multiple events each semester. These meetings range from icebreakers and games to deeper discussions. We also bring in speakers and performers to further advance cultural, social, political, and historical awareness (and for fun). ASA hopes to be a space where you can come relax and enjoy some friendly company.
