Book Archive

Here is a full list of the books I've read this year with approximate categories:

Books that significantly impacted my life:

  • Grit (Angela Duckworth)
  • The Life-changing Magic of Tidying Up (Marie Kondo)
  • Difficult Conversations (Douglas Stone, Bruce Patton, Sheila Heen)

Other books that I enjoyed:

  • Kafka on the Shore (Haruki Murakami)
  • Becoming (Michelle Obama)
  • Educated (Tara Westover)
  • Never Split the Difference (Chriss Voss and Tahl Raz)
  • Bad Blood (John Carreyrou)
  • When Breath Becomes Air (Paul Kalanithi)

Books I thought were okay:

  • Inspired: How to create tech products people love (Marty Cagan)
  • Outliers (Malcolm Gladwell)
  • Shoe Dog (Phil Knight)
  • Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind (Yuval Noah Harari)

Books I would not recommend:

  • The Emperor of All Maladies (Siddhartha Mukherjee)
  • Atomic Habits (James Clear)
  • The Gene (Siddhartha Mukherjee)
  • Good for You, Great for Me (Lawrence Susskind)

I'm always happy to talk about the books that I've read and explain why I liked or disliked them! If you'd like to discuss any of these books or give me recommendations for more books, feel free to reach out to me!
