52 Books in 52 Weeks

In 2019, I resolved to read one book every week. Of the books I've read this year, here are the ones that really changed my life:

  • Grit (Angela Duckworth)
  • The Life-changing Magic of Tidying Up (Marie Kondo)
  • Difficult Conversations (Douglas Stone, Bruce Patton, and Sheila Heen)
  • Thanks for the Feedback (Douglas Stone and Sheila Heen)
  • Lean In (Sheryl Sandberg)

Other books I'd recommend:

  • The Better Angels of Our Nature (Steven Pinker)
  • Homo Deus (Yuval Noah Harari)
  • Kafka on the Shore (Haruki Murakami)
  • Becoming (Michelle Obama)
  • Educated (Tara Westover)
  • Never Split the Difference (Chriss Voss and Tahl Raz)
  • Bad Blood (John Carreyrou)

I also have an archive of the complete list of books I've read this year. There are many other great books that I've read and would recommend, even if they didn't make this page!

I'm always happy to talk about the books! If you'd like to discuss, feel free to reach out to me!
