PM Intern at MIT Horizon

Public Website

MIT Horizon is a content library dedicated to helping people learn about emerging technologies. It is part of MIT Open Learning, and is set to launch in Spring 2019.

During my junior year, I worked as a product manager intern at MIT Horizon. As our fourth employee, it was incredible watching the product grow from an idea to fruition!

During my time there, some of the things that I worked on included creating our public facing marketing website, redesigning the topic page, working closely with our development team to create the product, and creating a detailed workflow for user registration and single-sign on that is both user friendly and GDPR compliant.

I'm incredibly excited to see where MIT Horizon goes in the future, and suggest you check it out if you're interested in learning about a broad range of emerging technologies!
